## What Exactly Is RadoNet Selling?

The short version: VPS instances in Japan (Tokyo and Yokohama) and Hong Kong, running on real ISP broadband lines — the kind your local internet provider would give you at home or in an office. Not a traditional datacenter IP block.

Why does that matter? Because streaming services like Netflix and Disney+ can't casually blacklist entire residential ISP ranges without killing service for millions of actual subscribers. So these IPs stay clean. They unlock content that datacenter VPS addresses get bounced from constantly.

## Real Performance Numbers

Testing data from the Hong Kong HGC line shows:

- **China Mobile:** Sustained ~100 Mbps throughput, latency 44–74 ms. Direct routing, no detours. Consistently the best performer.
- **China Unicom:** Downloads 78–91 Mbps, latency ~68–73 ms via South Korea KT routing.

For Japan So-net residential: Geekbench 6 scored 1,384 single-core and 2,534 multi-core on the EPYC 7K62 at 2.6 GHz. International latency from Hong Kong runs 43 ms to Singapore, 154 ms to LA, 244 ms to France — exactly what you'd expect from an Asia-based server.
